Inspiration Flyer.

This was a project done under the Graphic Stories theme of "The Power of Youth". This poster represents the diversity and creativity of the upcoming generation of youth, and a hope for the innovation and progress we'll see in the future as we work with them to make our world a better place. While incorporating this message, I also kept in mind to focus on artistic minimalism and abstraction for the design focus. This gives the poster a polished, modern look that is both classing and trending.
