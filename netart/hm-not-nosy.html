Neither of you talk.

You look out the window.

He looks at you.

He does not stop looking at you.

The train passes over something, a bump, or a very big rock, and you balance yourself.

The man does not balance himself. He tips forward and his hat falls off.

There is a rat under his hat.


The rat looks at you.

You look at the rat.


The rat smiles, wider and wider, until it is more smile than rat.

The smile asks you a question.


Which one of your lives is this?
