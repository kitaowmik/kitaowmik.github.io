When I wake up it's because he's kicking my foot.

He kicks my leg again, and his foot moves like its being pulled by a string.

I move back. "I'm awake."

"Your stop." His mouth is still moving very strangely.

The train stops.

I get up.

"Thank you," I tell him.

As I leave the compartment, I see the woman in the one across. She is pressed against the glass, staring at me.

She smiles.


I quickly exit the train.
