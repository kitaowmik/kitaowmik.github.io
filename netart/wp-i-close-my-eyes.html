I wake up. I can't believe I fell asleep. I was just trying not to look at her teeth.

The train is still moving. Of course it is.

The lady is still there. She pulls a bit of fur out of her mouth.

She sees me staring. "The coat is shedding," she explains. "Did you sleep well?"

"I did."

"That's nice. You slept for a long time."

"Did I miss my stop?" That would be bad. Mostly because I don't want to be around her teeth any longer.

"You said it's the one after the next?"

"Yes."

"Then no."

"Where are we now? Is my stop next?"

"The train hasn't stopped at all."

"That's strange. I feel like I slept hours." I don't know why I keep talking. She keeps talking back, so I keep seeing bits of her stupid teeth."

"You did." She checks her watch. "You slept two hours."

"Then I missed my stop."

I just told you the train hasn't stopped."

"But if it's been two hours-"

"I told you." She is smiling again. "The train has not stopped."

Why did the train not stop?
