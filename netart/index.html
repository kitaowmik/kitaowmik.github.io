Just a train ride.

The train stops. I see all the carriages ahead get full, so I go to the emptiest one. It's the kitchen carriage, and there are only three compartments.

The train starts moving, and I stumble. I have to sit somewhere.

One compartment on the left has a woman in a fur coat. I sit there.

One compartment on the right has a man in a top hat. I sit there.

I have a strange feeling, that I am stuck in a dream. Can I go back home?
