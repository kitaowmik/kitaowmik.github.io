"It's pretty cold, isn't it?"

Her smile freezes, and for a second, I think something is wrong.

But then she says, "yes, it has been getting colder at night now."

"I'm scared I'll fall asleep and miss my stop," I admit.

"What's your stop?"

"The next one after this."

"I'll wake you up," she offers."

I think this is nice, and I thank her.

She starts to smile again, and I stop thinking she is nice.

I close my eyes so I don't have to see all her teeth.

I try to smile back.
